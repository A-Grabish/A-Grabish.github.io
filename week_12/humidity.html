Latitude vs Humidity

There is an obsevable correlation between the current temperature of a city and its proximity to the Equator. A limitation of this dataset is that it is a snapshot of today's weather (August 8th), and not an average of an entire year. The Summer Solstice occured on June 21 this year, so the Earth's Northern Hemisphere is tilted towards the sun. This is reflected in the Temperature graph as the warmest area is near the Tropic of Cancer (23.4687 degrees Latitude), not at the Equator.
